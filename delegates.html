Delegate Assembly

The delegates representing each member club at the Southwest Division Delegate Assembly for the 2026 season.

Every member club appoints delegates to the SWD Delegate Assembly, which approves division policy, the tournament program, and governance. To get involved, reach out to your club's delegates or the club directly.
